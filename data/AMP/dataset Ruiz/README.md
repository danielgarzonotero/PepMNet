**Ruiz Puentes, P.; Henao, M. C.; Cifuentes, J.; Muñoz-Camargo, C.; Reyes, L. H.; Cruz, J. C.; Arbeláez, P.**  
*Rational Discovery of Antimicrobial Peptides by Means of Artificial Intelligence.* Membranes 2022, 12 (7), 708.
